About Data Acquisition System Department
The department was founded by doctor of sciences, professor A.I. Kondalev in 1963, who was at the head till 1989. There are 21 scientists in the department now including 4 Doctors of sciences, and 4 PhDs.
Now the department is headed by doctor of sciences, professor V.O. Romanov.
Volodymyr ROMANOV, Head of department, Doctor of technical sciences, Professor. He was awarded Academician Lebedev Prize, Honorary degree Inventor of the Year of Ukraine, awarded State Prize of Ukraine in Science and Technique, and Honorary degree Honoured Scientist of Ukraine.
